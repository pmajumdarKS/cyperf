* `STEPUPSEGMENT` (value: `'StepUpSegment'`)

* `STEPDOWNSEGMENT` (value: `'StepDownSegment'`)

* `EMPTY` (value: `''`)

* `NORMALSEGMENT` (value: `'NormalSegment'`)

* `RAMPDOWNSEGMENT` (value: `'RampDownSegment'`)

* `IDLESEGMENT` (value: `'IdleSegment'`)
